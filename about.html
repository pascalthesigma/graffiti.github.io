About Graffiti Program

Learn about our mission, vision, and how we support creators

Our Mission

The Graffiti Program was created to break down barriers and give smaller content creators the recognition they deserve. Unlike more restrictive programs, we focus on raw talent, creativity, and potential rather than existing follower counts.

As former small creators ourselves, we understand the challenges of growing a channel and building an audience. That's why we've built a program that prioritizes genuine talent and dedication over arbitrary metrics.

Through our community-driven approach, we're creating a space where emerging creators can thrive, collaborate, and grow together.

Our Story

2023

The Beginning

After observing the limitations and barriers in Gorilla Tag's Finger Painter program, our team decided to create a more inclusive platform for smaller creators.

June 2023

Foundation

Under the leadership of Kooper and Garrison, with Pascal as Head Staff, we established the core principles and structure of the Graffiti Program.

July 2023

Team Assembly

Our dedicated staff team was formed, including Javaa, GIDDYGT, HarveyGT, Maybe_gtag, MVPRINCE, Nicebot, and Lyairr, each bringing unique expertise to support our creators.

Today

Growing Community

The Graffiti Program continues to expand, providing opportunities and support to deserving content creators, emphasizing quality and creativity over follower count.

Our Values

Community

We believe in the power of community and collaborative growth. By bringing creators together, we foster an environment where everyone can learn, share, and thrive.

Creativity

We celebrate unique perspectives and innovative approaches to content creation. Original thinking and artistic expression are at the heart of what we do.

Inclusivity

We welcome creators from all backgrounds, cultures, and experience levels. Diversity strengthens our community and enriches the content we create.

Quality

We encourage our members to strive for excellence in their craft, focusing on continuous improvement and dedication to their unique vision.

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I join the Graffiti Program?

To join the Graffiti Program, you need to submit an application through our website. We review applications on a quarterly basis and select creators based on their content quality, uniqueness, and potential for growth.

What kind of creators are you looking for?

We welcome creators from all niches and platforms, including YouTube, Twitch, TikTok, and more. We value original content, consistency, passion, and a genuine desire to grow and contribute to the community.

Is there a minimum follower requirement?

No, we don't have strict follower requirements. The Graffiti Program is specifically designed to support smaller creators with potential. We evaluate applications based on content quality and creator commitment rather than audience size.

How long can I stay in the program?

There is no set time limit for participation. As long as you remain active in the community and continue creating content, you can remain a member. We do conduct periodic reviews to ensure all members are actively participating.

Does the program cost anything to join?

The Graffiti Program is completely free to join. We believe in removing barriers to entry for talented creators, not adding them.

What is the relationship with Gorilla AG?

The Graffiti Program operates as an initiative within Gorilla AG, focusing on discovering and nurturing emerging creative talent within the broader community.

How are you different from other creator programs?

Unlike other programs that might focus heavily on metrics or existing audience size, we evaluate creators based on their potential, creativity, and dedication. We believe that great content deserves recognition, regardless of current follower count.

How long has the program been running?

The Graffiti Program was founded in mid-2023 as a response to the need for a more inclusive creator support system. While we're a young program, our team brings together extensive experience in content creation and community building.

Who runs the Graffiti Program?

The program is led by Kooper (Owner) and Garrison (Co-Owner), with Pascal serving as Head Staff. Our dedicated staff team includes Javaa, GIDDYGT, HarveyGT, Maybe_gtag, MVPRINCE, Nicebot, and Lyairr, who work together to support our community members.
