Our Mission

The Graffiti Program is dedicated to discovering and nurturing talented content creators within the Gorilla Tag community. We believe that everyone deserves a chance to shine, regardless of their current following.

Our mission is to provide resources, support, and opportunities to help creators grow their channels and improve their content quality while fostering a positive and collaborative community.

Our Story

The Graffiti Program was founded with a simple yet powerful idea: to create a platform that recognizes and supports talented content creators in the Gorilla Tag community who might otherwise go unnoticed.

Starting as a small initiative, we've grown into a thriving community of creators who support and inspire each other. Our program has helped numerous content creators improve their skills, grow their audience, and achieve their creative goals.
