Why Apply?

The Graffiti Program is designed to support and promote smaller YouTubers and content creators by providing resources, mentorship, and a supportive community. As a member, you'll gain:

  • Community Support

    Connect with like-minded creators who understand your journey

  • Increased Exposure

    Get featured on our platform and social media channels

  • Professional Growth

    Access workshops, tutorials, and mentorship opportunities

  • Collaboration Opportunities

    Partner with other members on creative projects

What We Look For

While we don't have strict follower count requirements, we do look for creators who demonstrate:

  • Quality content with attention to detail and production value
  • Creativity and originality in your approach to content creation
  • Consistency in posting schedule and content style
  • Passion for your content niche
  • Community engagement and how you interact with your audience
  • Collaborative spirit and willingness to support fellow creators
  • Growth potential and dedication to improving your craft

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the application process take?

The review process typically takes 2-3 weeks. If your application progresses to the interview stage, you can expect to hear from us within a month of submission.

Is there a minimum follower requirement?

No, we don't have strict follower requirements. We evaluate applications based on content quality, creativity, consistency, and growth potential rather than audience size.

What happens if my application is rejected?

We provide feedback on all applications, and you're welcome to reapply after 3 months. Many current members were accepted on their second or third application after implementing our feedback.
