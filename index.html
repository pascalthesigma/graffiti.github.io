The Graffiti Program supports and promotes talented content creators who deserve more recognition. We offer resources, mentorship, and a supportive community to help them grow.

What is the Graffiti Program?

The Graffiti Program is a program in Gorilla Tag that functions similarly to Finger Painter Program but is specifically designed to support and promote smaller YouTubers and content creators who might not otherwise get the visibility they deserve.

We believe that creativity should be accessible to everyone, regardless of follower count or platform status. Our mission is to discover, nurture, and promote emerging talent in the digital content creation space.

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of creators do you accept?

We accept creators who focus on Gorilla Tag content across YouTube (long-form videos and Shorts), and TikTok. Your content must be original and focused on the Gorilla Tag community.

Is there a minimum follower requirement?

No, we don't have strict follower requirements. We evaluate applications based on content quality, creativity, consistency, and growth potential rather than audience size.

How can I apply to join?

Visit our application page and fill out the form. Applications open on the first day of each month at midnght!

Is there a cost to join the program?

The Graffiti Program is completely free to join. We believe in removing barriers to entry for talented creators.

What benefits do members receive?

Members receive community support, increased exposure, professional growth opportunities, collaboration possibilities with other creators, and access to exclusive resources. And of course the amazing Graffiti Badge.

How long does the application process take?

The review process typically takes about a week.

What makes Graffiti Program different?

Unlike other programs that focus on metrics or existing audience size, we evaluate creators based on their potential, creativity, and dedication. We believe great content deserves recognition, regardless of follower count.

How do you help creators grow?

We provide networking opportunities, promotional features, constructive feedback, resources, and a supportive community to help creators expand their audience and improve their content quality.
